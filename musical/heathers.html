Heathers: The Musical is a rock musical with music, lyrics, and a book by Laurence O'Keefe and Kevin Murphy and based on the 1988 cult film Heathers. the show opens conversations about dark issues including 'bullying, teen suicide, and violence in schools. The 'social politicsof high school are shifted when rebellious Veronica Sawyer meets the damaged romantic wild card J.D., and the hierarchy ruled by the clique of Heathers falls apart with accidental and not so accidental murders. Heathers explores empowerment and vulnerability in the relationships between friends, lovers, parents and school communities, creating a show of high emotion.
